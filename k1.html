Kavin’s Line-up Looks Promising!

By M9
Updated 14:02 July 09, 2024
We are M9, use this new URL M9.news for latest updates.

Kavin is one of the emerging actors in Kollywood who has been critically acclaimed for his acting in his previous movies, such as Dada and Star. He is going to be the next big thing in Kollywood, as his line-up looks promising.

Kavin is currently awaiting the release of his next movie, Bloody Begger, directed by debutant M. Sivabalan, who is one of the assistant directors of Nelson Dilipkumar. This movie was planned to release in the month of August. Parallelly, he almost completed the shooting of his next movie, Kiss, directed by popular choreographer Sathish. The music for this movie was composed by Anirudh Ravichander, and the team is planning to release this movie for Diwali.

On the other hand, he is also acting in a movie titled Mask, directed by Vikram Ashokan. Vetrimaaran is producing this movie, and the music for this movie was composed by GV Prakash Kumar. This film is said to be a thriller and will not have any songs in it. These are the confirmed projects of Kavin. As per speculation, he also signed a film directed by Vishnu Eddavan, who is one of the assistant directors of Lokesh Kanagaraj. This movie is set to be produced by Seven Screen Studios, and he is pairing up with Nayanthara in this movie.




Looking up the line-ups of Kavin, one thing is sure: he is going to be the next big thing in Kollywood, and also, his previous movies, such as Dada and Star, have had a great box office performance. This clearly indicates that the sign of a star is rising in Kollywood.
